Non-negotiable rules

1
Consent firstTransactional and optional communication categories stay separate. SMS requires explicit consent and opt-out handling.
2
Clinical reviewDischarge instructions come from approved visit content. Automation never invents medical advice.
3
Secure linksNo diagnoses, full account numbers, policy numbers, or detailed records in SMS.
4
Reliable schedulingDatabase-backed outbox, idempotency keys, retries, quiet hours, cancellation on state change.
5
Human escalationNegative response opens owned work with priority, deadline, context, and completion audit.
6
Safe rolloutKeep production sends disabled. Test first with the provided QA email, then approved phones, then a small live cohort.
7
PIMS-ready boundaryClinic, provider, branding, and workflow settings stay configurable for Cornerstone, AVImark, and other PIMS adapters.
8
Role ownershipAdmin owns clinic settings, veterinarians own clinical tasks, and staff owns front-desk work. Enforce this server-side.
